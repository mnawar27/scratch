Here are some of the University of Delaware student
organizations that might be of interest to you!


  • Arabic Language Club

    • The Arabic Language Club was founded to celebrate and

    promote Arabic language and culture, no language experience required.

  • Food and Culinary Club

    • For foodies, future cooks, or students just looking to learn some basic cooking skills,

    this club is for you! Our club with provide you with lists of ingredients that you will need for whatever we are making

    for that zoom meeting! Although we can’t all be together cooking in the Willard food lab, we will do what we can over zoom!

  • Japanese Culture and Language Club

    • The JPLC welcomes all students who are interested in Japanese, not just those

    who are taking Japanese classes! During each of their meetings,

    they do a v ariety of activities relating to Japanese culture and language to help members learn and hone their skills.

  • Muslim Student Association

    • The UD MSA is committed to informing Muslims and non-Muslims about the traditional teachings of

    Islam through routine organized events and programming.

  • Zumba Club

    • Ditch the workout- join the club! Zumba is a latin-inspired, easy-to-follow, calorie burning, dance

    fitness party! The Zumba Club offers FREE ZUMBA classes 6 days a week plus jam packed

    “Zumbathons” available to ALL UD students! Add our club for times!
